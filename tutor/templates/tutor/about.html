About Keyboard Quest

My Mission

I made Keyboard Quest to help Software Engineering students improve typing speed, accuracy, and confidence while also learning technical vocabulary in a simple and engaging way.

The game is designed to start with easier typing practice and gradually move into more difficult words, sentences, and paragraphs, so students can build skills over time.
